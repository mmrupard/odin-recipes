Lasagna

Lasagna is a baked pasta dish containing sheets of pasta, ground meat, and tomato sauce

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

  • Ground Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna noodles
  5. Cover in foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest
